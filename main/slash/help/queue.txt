__Queue Commands__:
  Commands:  **list, remove, move, shuffle, clear**
  **List** - Displays all songs in the queue **(.queue list)**
    -Use the arrow reactions to cycle through pages
  **Remove** - Deletes a song from a given position in the queue **(.queue remove 2)**
    -Use list to find a song's queue position
  **Move** - Moves a song from one position to another **(.queue move 11 2)**
  **Shuffle** - Rearranges the queue in random order **(.queue shuffle)**
    -The first song in the queue will always stay in place to avoid playing it twice
  **Clear** - Removes all songs from the queue **(.queue clear)**
