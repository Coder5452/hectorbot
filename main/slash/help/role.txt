__Role Commands__:
  Usage:  **add, remove, list**
  **Add** - Adds a role to check for **(.role add @DJ)**
    -If no roles are added, anyone can use the bot
    -There's a limit of 5 roles to avoid slowing the bot
  **Remove** - Removes a role to check for  **(.role remove 2)**
    -Use list to find the numbers to remove a role
  **List** - Provides a list of all roles **(.role list)**
